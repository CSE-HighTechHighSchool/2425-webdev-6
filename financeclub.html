About Our Club

MoneyMindsNJ Finance Club brings together students passionate about financial literacy, investing, and personal money management. Each week, we post new articles, resources, and posters to keep our members and the community informed about the world of finance.

Our Mission: Encourage discussions, share insights, and foster a network of peers who support one another’s financial growth.
