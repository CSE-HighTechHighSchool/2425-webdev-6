Our Mission

At EcoSmart, we aim to revolutionize waste management by integrating smart technologies and community engagement. Our goal is to reduce waste, promote recycling, and make our cities cleaner and more sustainable for future generations.

Our Journey

  • 2018

    EcoSmart was founded with a vision to tackle urban waste challenges using technology.

  • 2019

    Launched our first smart waste bins equipped with sensors and data analytics.

  • 2020

    Expanded our services to include AI-powered recycling programs and community initiatives.

  • 2021

    Partnered with local governments to implement city-wide waste reduction strategies.

  • 2022

    Introduced our user-friendly app for waste tracking and scheduling pickups.
